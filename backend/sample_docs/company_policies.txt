# Company Policies and Guidelines

## Customer Support Policy

### Response Time Standards

Our commitment to customers is built on timely and effective support:

**Priority Levels:**
1. **Critical (P1)**: Service down or major functionality broken
   - Response time: 15 minutes
   - Resolution target: 4 hours
   - Escalation: Immediate to senior engineer

2. **High (P2)**: Significant feature not working, workaround available
   - Response time: 2 hours
   - Resolution target: 24 hours
   - Escalation: Every 12 hours

3. **Medium (P3)**: Minor issues, general questions
   - Response time: 8 hours
   - Resolution target: 5 business days
   - Escalation: Every 3 days

4. **Low (P4)**: Feature requests, enhancement suggestions
   - Response time: 24 hours
   - Resolution target: Best effort
   - Review: Monthly planning

### Customer Communication Standards

**Tone and Language:**
- Always be professional, friendly, and empathetic
- Use clear, jargon-free language
- Personalize responses with customer's name
- Acknowledge frustration and apologize for issues
- Provide specific timelines, not vague promises

**Required Information:**
Every response must include:
- Clear explanation of the issue
- Steps taken to resolve
- Next actions required (if any)
- Expected resolution timeline
- Contact information for follow-up

### Refund and Cancellation Policy

**Refund Eligibility:**
- Full refund within 30 days of purchase
- Pro-rated refund for annual plans if canceled mid-year
- No refund for enterprise custom development work
- Service credit for verified downtime exceeding SLA

**Cancellation Process:**
1. Customer submits cancellation request
2. Account manager contacts within 24 hours
3. Exit interview to understand reasons
4. Data export provided within 48 hours
5. Account deactivated at end of billing period
6. Data retained for 90 days before permanent deletion

### Data Privacy and GDPR Compliance

**Data Collection:**
We collect only essential data:
- Account information (name, email, company)
- Usage analytics (anonymized)
- Support conversation history
- Payment information (via PCI-compliant processor)

**Customer Rights:**
- Right to access personal data
- Right to data portability
- Right to be forgotten (deletion)
- Right to rectification
- Right to restrict processing

**Data Retention:**
- Active accounts: Indefinite
- Canceled accounts: 90 days
- Support tickets: 7 years (legal requirement)
- Analytics: 2 years (aggregated)

### Service Level Agreement (SLA)

**Uptime Guarantee:**
- 99.95% uptime for Professional plans
- 99.99% uptime for Enterprise plans
- Scheduled maintenance windows: Monthly, 2AM-4AM UTC

**SLA Credits:**
If we fail to meet uptime commitments:
- 99.00% - 99.95%: 10% credit
- 95.00% - 99.00%: 25% credit
- Below 95.00%: 50% credit

**Exclusions:**
SLA does not cover:
- Issues caused by customer's infrastructure
- Third-party service failures
- DDoS attacks or security incidents
- Force majeure events
- Scheduled maintenance

## Employee Guidelines

### Customer Interaction Standards

**Do's:**
✓ Listen actively to customer concerns
✓ Take ownership of issues
✓ Follow up proactively
✓ Document all interactions thoroughly
✓ Escalate when appropriate
✓ Maintain confidentiality

**Don'ts:**
✗ Make promises you can't keep
✗ Argue with customers
✗ Blame other departments
✗ Share confidential information
✗ Close tickets without resolution
✗ Ignore negative feedback

### Escalation Procedures

**When to Escalate:**
- Customer requests manager
- Issue exceeds your expertise
- Security or legal concerns
- VIP/Enterprise customer issues
- Potential PR crisis
- Repeated complaints about same issue

**How to Escalate:**
1. Document all relevant information
2. Notify team lead via Slack
3. Create escalation ticket
4. Brief the receiving team member
5. Follow up to ensure handoff
6. Update customer on escalation

### Quality Assurance

**Ticket Review Process:**
- 10% of tickets randomly reviewed weekly
- All P1/P2 tickets reviewed
- Customer satisfaction surveys sent after resolution
- Monthly team performance reviews
- Quarterly training based on common issues

**Performance Metrics:**
- Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT): Target > 90%
- First Response Time: Target < SLA
- Resolution Time: Target < SLA
- Ticket Reopen Rate: Target < 5%
- Knowledge Base Usage: Track and improve

## Security Policies

### Incident Response Plan

**Detection:**
- 24/7 monitoring of all systems
- Automated alerting for anomalies
- Regular security audits
- Customer-reported issues

**Response:**
1. Identify and verify incident
2. Activate incident response team
3. Contain and isolate affected systems
4. Investigate root cause
5. Remediate and recover
6. Document and review

**Communication:**
- Internal: Immediate notification to leadership
- Customers: Within 72 hours of confirmed breach
- Regulatory: As required by law (GDPR, etc.)
- Public: Only after customer notification complete

### Access Control

**Principle of Least Privilege:**
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Regular access reviews (quarterly)
- Immediate revocation upon termination
- Multi-factor authentication required
- Production access logged and monitored

**Data Classification:**
- Public: Marketing materials
- Internal: Business operations data
- Confidential: Customer data
- Restricted: Financial, legal, security data

## Billing and Payment Policies

### Payment Terms

**Accepted Methods:**
- Credit card (Visa, MasterCard, Amex)
- ACH/Bank transfer (Enterprise only)
- Purchase orders (Enterprise only)
- PayPal (Starter/Professional only)

**Billing Cycles:**
- Monthly: Charged on same day each month
- Annual: 20% discount, charged once yearly
- Enterprise: Custom terms negotiable

**Late Payments:**
- Grace period: 7 days
- First reminder: Day 8
- Service suspension: Day 15
- Account termination: Day 30
- Reactivation fee: $50

### Invoicing

**Invoice Details:**
All invoices include:
- Company name and billing address
- Invoice number and date
- Itemized charges
- Tax breakdown (if applicable)
- Payment instructions
- Due date

**Disputes:**
- Must be raised within 30 days
- Email billing@aiservice.com
- Provide detailed explanation
- Response within 5 business days
- Resolution within 15 business days
